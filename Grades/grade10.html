Grade 10

1984

By George Orwell

Genre: Political Fiction, Dystopian

In a world where everything is monitored by the government, where every action is mandated and where personal privacy does not exist, a single man commits a crime. He begins to think. Winston Smith begins to write a dairy. As his dairy's size increases, so does his treason. How long can he escape?

Big Brother is Watching You.
