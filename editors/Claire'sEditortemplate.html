Claire Honeycutt

Intense love does not measure. It just gives. ~Mother Theresa

Age: 17

Home Town: Round Rock

HomeState: Texas

Favourite Genre: Fantasy Romance (not the sparkly vampires, just the mermaids and dragons and cool knights)

Favourite Author: J.R.R. Tolkien

Where I like to read: In the parlor with my favourite mug of tea and a bar of dark chocolate.

These are some of my favourite books (or series) that I would reccommend reading:

  1. The Healer's Apprentice by Melanie Dickerson (honest opinion: read the entire series. Melanie is brilliant and her characters shine.)
  2. The Unicorn Chronicles by Bruce Coville (Unicorns, cute squirrel-ish creatures, and giant teddy bears with lots of hair, and a teenage girl fight against a mean old woman who wants to kill all the unicorns.)
  3. The Inheritance Cycle by Christopher Paolini (Dragons and dragon-riders. If I wasn't in love with my horse, I would want a dragon. No, scratch that. I do want a dragon.)
  4. The Pimpernelle's by Patricia Elliott (French Revolution- young lady gets caught up in politics and running from le Fantome)
  5. The Mortal Instruments by Cassandra Clare (Demons, demon-hunters, fairies, and sparkly warlocks fight against an evil ex-Shadowhunter who wants to rule the world.)
