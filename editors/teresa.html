Teresa Graves

Why, I’m just as true and honest as dirt. And I’m even more charming than dirt. ~Aly from "Trickster's Choice"

Age: 17 years

Home Town: Post Falls

HomeState: Idaho

Favorite Genre: Fantasy fiction

Favorite Author: I don't have a single favorite but these are my most read authors: Tamora Pierce, Diana Wayne Jones, Patricia C. Wrede, John Flanagan, and Shakespeare.

Where I like to read: I like to read on my bed or on the couch or on my window seat. Honestly, anywhere that I am not disturbed. I call these places my 'black holes.'

These are some of my favourite books that I would reccommend reading:

  1. Howl's Moving Castle by Diana Wayne Jones (and sequels)
  2. The Chrestomanci Chronicles(series) by Diana Wayne Jones
  3. The Ranger's Apprentice(series) by John Flanagan
  4. The Enchanted Forest Chronicles(series) by Patricia C. Wrede
  5. Snow White and Rose Red by Patricia C. Wrede
  6. Trickster's Choice(duet) by Tamora Pierce
  7. Wild Magic(quartet) by Tamora Pierce
  8. Posse of Princesses by Sherwood Smith
  9. The Land of Elyon(quintet) by Patrick Carmen
